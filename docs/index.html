About Us

IASD was founded in the Bay Area in 2019 by Leila Natochy and Hannah Yawar. We are dedicated to supporting refugees and immigrants that came to the United States. Our mission is to build a community of people from different backgrounds that strive to achieve social justice and help protest through organized meetings.

Future Goals

  • Build a donation organization or protest against discrimination
  • Educate people about the unfairness of being the target of exploitation and wage theft
  • Raise awareness through social media, putting others into perspective of the challenges immigrants face

How to Get Involved

Join us in our mission by volunteering, attending events, or becoming a member. By volunteering, you can directly support our programs and initiatives that aim to create a more inclusive community. Attending our events gives you the opportunity to learn, share, and network with others who are passionate about social justice. Becoming a member allows you to stay connected, receive updates on our efforts, and be a part of a movement that is making a huge difference. Your involvement, no matter how big or small, plays a vital role in helping us create lasting change. Click the button below to sign up and take the first step toward making a real impact.
